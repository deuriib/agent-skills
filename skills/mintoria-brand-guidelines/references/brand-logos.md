# Mintoria Logo Guidelines

The Mintoria logo represents the intersection of digital assets and traditional financial stability.

## Logo Assets

Assets are located in the `assets/` directory of this skill.

- `mintoria-logo.webp`: The full brand logo.
- `mintoria-icon.webp`: The standalone icon/mark.

## Usage Guidelines

### 1. Logo Composition

The standard logo composition consists of the **Mintoria Icon** followed by the wordmark **"Mintoria"**.

- **Icon**: Should be displayed with a slight rotation on hover (e.g., `group-hover:rotate-12`).
- **Typography**: The wordmark uses font weight `font-black` (900) with `tracking-tight` letter spacing.

### 2. Sizing

- **Navigation Bar**: The icon should be roughly `40px` to `48px` in height.
- **Footer**: The icon should be slightly smaller, or consistent with the brand mark.

### 3. Backgrounds

- The logo is designed to work on both `var(--color-surface)` (light) and dark backgrounds.
- For dark backgrounds, ensure the typography is `var(--color-text-on-dark)`.

### 4. Interactive State

Hover effects on the logo should feel "alive":

- Subtle rotation of the icon.
- Slight lift or opacity change if part of a button.
